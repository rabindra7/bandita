About Aroksha

We "Aroksha", coming to you all with a main motive of online counselling related to various diseases, a guidance and facility of arranging clinical tests of senior citizens and others from the home itself those who are physically weak and challenged. We will also arrange online dietician, doctor for endocrine and doctor’s consultation. Apart from the above, we also provide the service of free diet counselling and doctors on available at various places such as old age homes, colleges, villages, tea tribe areas, orphanage and gym etc. We are also having collaborations in various segments. We always looking forward to you and "Aroksha" believes in service for humanity. Aroksha’s medical practitioners are always ready to solve your problems.
