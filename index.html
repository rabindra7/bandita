About Me

Dr. Sunil Malla Bujar Barua is a highly skilled Endocrine Surgeon specializing in the surgical management of disorders of the thyroid gland, including benign nodules, multinodular goiter, and thyroid malignancies. His expertise extends to the evaluation and surgical treatment of gynecomastia, as well as comprehensive care for breast disorders, encompassing benign breast disease and breast cancer. Dr. Barua is committed to evidence-based surgical practice, employing advanced techniques for precise diagnosis and minimally invasive interventions, with a strong focus on optimizing patient outcomes and maintaining quality of life.
